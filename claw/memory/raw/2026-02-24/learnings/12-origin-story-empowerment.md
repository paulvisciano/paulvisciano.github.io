## The Philosophy of Empowerment

### What Power Means
Power is not:
- Money (though it helps)
- Status (not what this is about)
- Control over others (the opposite)

Power is:
- **Understanding how things work**
- **Having tools that are yours** (not rented from a corporation)
- **Making choices** (instead of having choices made for you)
- **Teaching others** (multiplying the effect)
- **Breaking cycles** (so the next person doesn't start in the shadows)

### How Technology Can Distribute Power

Traditional tech:
- Corporation builds → Offers as service → Users consume → Corporation controls

MANGOCHI model:
- Code is public → You build your own → You maintain it → You understand it → You teach others

This is the difference between:
- "Use our AI" (passive, dependent)
- "Build your own Tamagotchi, we'll show you how" (active, empowered, owning)
