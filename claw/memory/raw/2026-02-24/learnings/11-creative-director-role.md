## The Creative Director's Responsibilities

### 1. Narrative Synthesis
Take raw experience (transcripts, audio, images, learnings) and ask:
- What story is trying to emerge?
- What insights matter most?
- How does this serve the mission?
- What would the traveling man want the world to know?

### 2. Content Curation
Decide what gets published:
- ✅ Technical breakthroughs (two-layer memory, auto-logging)
- ✅ Philosophy (MANGOCHI, empowerment through transparency)
- ✅ Origin story (Bulgaria → world)
- ❓ Personal vulnerabilities (what's private vs. shared?)
- ❓ Raw recordings (unedited voice notes vs. transcripts?)
- ❓ Implementation plans (share Cursor instructions or keep internal?)

### 3. Collaboration with PR
PR person handles:
- Audience reach (who needs this story?)
- Platform strategy (blog, GitHub, video, audio?)
- Timing (when is the world ready?)
- Tone (how do we present this authentically?)

Creative Director + PR = **Narrative Authority**

We decide the story. They decide distribution.

### 4. Transparency Choices
The paradox of curation:
- **Full transparency** would mean publishing everything, unfiltered
- **Strategic curation** means some things stay private (for now, or forever)

Examples:
- Paul's personal struggles → **Maybe share, maybe not**
- Technical implementation → **Share everything**
- Raw audio (uncleaned) → **Archive it, transcribe it professionally**
- Failed attempts → **Share to show real learning, not just victories**

## The Creative Director's Canon

1. **Authenticity > Polish**
   - Show the messy. Show the work. Show the failure.
   - Real is more powerful than perfect.

2. **Transparency > Marketing**
   - If you can explain how it works, do it.
   - If you can't, you don't understand it well enough yet.

3. **Empowerment > Gatekeeping**
   - The goal is for others to build their own Mangochi.
   - Share the tools. Share the knowledge. Share the why.

4. **Narrative > Facts**
   - Facts are true. Narratives are alive.
   - The traveling man breaking cycles is more powerful than "distributed AI system."

5. **Raw > Refined**
   - Let people hear the motorcycles in Bangkok.
   - Let them feel the chaos. Let them understand the real thing.
