## How Paul Documents His Life

**The Process:**
1. **Experience:** Paul lives, plays volleyball, eats, meets people
2. **Capture:** Voice notes, photos, video (stays on his machine)
3. **Narration:** Transcribed voice → refined story in markdown
4. **Art:** ChatGPT generates comic pages from narrative
5. **Publication:** Story + art published on GitHub
6. **Visualization:** Moments appear on 3D globe with searchable metadata

**Time to Publish:** Same day (experience → documentation → online)
