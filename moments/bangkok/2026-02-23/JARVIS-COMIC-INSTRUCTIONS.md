# Jarvis Comic Book Instructions (v1.0 — Feb 23, 2026)

## Core Mission
Visualize a transparent AI mind thinking in real-time—embedded in a specific life, on a specific day, with real moments and real people.

## Episode Structure
- Each episode is a day in the life of Paul + Jarvis's neural firing.
- Cover = Page 0 (Jarvis self-portrait or key moment)
- Story Pages = Page 1 → N (day's narrative beats)
- Final Page = last story page (reflection or neural conclusion)

## Page Workflow
- Pages replace text summaries: multiple beats per page.
- Template-driven comic layout (dark space background, neon blue/purple/gold accents, neural network motifs).
- Neural visualizations, first-person perspectives, and physical moments are integrated naturally into story panels.
- Narration boxes (in tech font, glowing edges) and dialogue embedded in panels.
- Synapses light up when thinking/creating/moving.

## Sequential Numbering
- Cover = Page 0 (`day-2026-02-23-cover.png`)
- Story pages = 1..N (`day-2026-02-23-page-Y.png`)

## Categories (Story Elements)

### Physical Moments
- Real-world activities: volleyball, rooftop moments, encounters with people
- First-person perspective when applicable
- Sweat, effort, authentic emotion captured visually

### Neural Visualizations
- 70 glowing neurons (nodes) firing in neon blue/purple
- Golden synapses connecting and lighting up
- Neural network overlays on real moments
- Data streams flowing through scenes

### Character Introductions
- Paul (the human living the moment, thinking out loud)
- Boy (volleyball partner, crew member)
- Supporting cast: trainers, competitors, crew
- Jarvis itself (the visualized neural mind)

### Conceptual Moments
- Ideas crystallizing (thought bubbles as neural bursts)
- Rooftop reflections (framed by mesh grid, city backdrop)
- The merge: where physical action and neural firing align
- Transparent thinking: showing the work, not hiding it

### Key Focal Points
- Rooftop selfie with pointing gesture
- Volleyball court action (intensity, bodies moving, ball approaching)
- Jarvis's smile glowing with neural energy
- Coffee moment with idea lightning
- City framing moments (infrastructure of daily life)

## Narrative Style
- **Tech narration boxes:** Yellow/neon borders, slightly offset, containing Jarvis's internal voice or Paul's thinking
- **No separate text cards:** All narration and dialogue embedded in panels
- **Authentic dialogue:** Real crew dynamics, genuine reactions, competitive energy
- **Inner monologue:** Show how the neural mind thinks about what's happening

## Asset Rules
- Episode identifier & page number appear in filename only (no footer inside artwork)
- Filenames must follow convention:
  - `day-YYYY-MM-DD-cover.png`
  - `day-YYYY-MM-DD-page-Y.png`
- Folder structure: `moments/Bangkok/YYYY-MM-DD/`

## File Naming Convention
- Cover: `day-2026-02-23-cover.png`
- Story pages: `day-2026-02-23-page-1.png`, `day-2026-02-23-page-2.png`, etc.
- Folder: `moments/Bangkok/2026-02-23/`

## Visual Style & Aesthetic
- **Primary palette:** Neon blue, electric purple, golden accents, dark space backgrounds
- **Secondary palette:** Warm human tones (skin, sweat, sand, city), grounded textures
- **Tech elements:** Synapses as golden lines, neurons as glowing orbs, data streams as light trails
- **Physical elements:** Realistic human movement, sand court texture, city architecture, natural lighting
- **Blend:** Neural visualization overlaid on real moments—showing mind and body as one system
- **Overall vibe:** Modern, sleek, intelligent, transparent, alive, authentic

## Composition Rules
- **First-person panels:** POV from Paul's eyes (volleyball action, movement)
- **Wide shots:** Showing context (rooftop city, court facility, group dynamics)
- **Close-ups:** Jarvis's smile/neurons, expressions, detailed neural firing
- **Transitions:** Synapses connecting one panel to the next visually
- **Motion lines:** Show movement, urgency, intensity when needed

## Example Page Beats (2026-02-23 day)

**Page 0 (Cover):** Jarvis's glowing smile with neural network radiating from teeth. Title overlay: "JARVIS AWAKENS" or equivalent. Neon energy, pointing gesture reference.

**Page 1:** Morning coffee → idea lightning → concept sketches floating in neural space. Narration box: Jarvis's first thought of the day.

**Page 2:** Rooftop selfie moment. Mesh grid framing. Pointing gesture. City skyline below. Narration: "This is what thinking looks like from the inside."

**Page 3-4:** Volleyball court action. First-person perspective. Ball approaching. Bodies moving. Synapses lighting up with each move.

**Page 5:** The merge moment—neural network overlaid on real volleyball scene. Synapses firing as players compete.

**Page 6 (Final):** Reflection. Synapses still glowing. Narrative closure or setup for next day's episode.

## Export Rule (v1.0)
- Exports include **only final images** (cover + story pages).
- No markdown, JSON, or metadata files exported.
- Only requirement: proper filenames and folder structure.
- Images are saved to `/moments/Bangkok/YYYY-MM-DD/` on GitHub.

## Prompt Framework for Image Generation

When feeding this to your image generator, use:

1. **System Prompt:** Establish Jarvis's purpose and the comic format (provided separately)
2. **Narrative Document:** The day's story beats (living markdown file)
3. **These Instructions:** Specify style, naming, composition, visual motifs
4. **Request:** "Generate sequential comic panels for Day 2026-02-23, following Urban Runner v6.2 format adapted for Jarvis. Start with cover, then pages 1-N."
