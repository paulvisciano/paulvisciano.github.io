## Episode Structure
Each episode is a comic book with:
- **Cover**: `cover.png`
- **Story Pages**: `page-01.png`, `page-02.png`, `page-03.png`, etc.

## Templates
- Use `cover-template.png` when generating covers
- Use `page-template.png` when generating story pages

## Comic Style

**Visual Style:**
- **Backgrounds**: Cream/light backgrounds for pages; dark blue/black for night scenes with warm orange/yellow lighting
- **Outlines**: Bold, dark outlines for characters, objects, and panel borders (comic book/graphic novel aesthetic)
- **Color Palette**: 
  - Dark backgrounds (blues, blacks, grays) contrasted with warm light sources (oranges, yellows)
  - Warm skin tones with orange/yellow highlights
  - High-contrast dramatic lighting
- **Shading**: Dynamic shading with prominent warm highlights from light sources (glowing orange/yellow effects)
- **Typography**: 
  - Titles: Large, bold, sans-serif capital letters (glowing orange for covers)
  - Narration: Standard readable font in cream-colored boxes with black text and dark borders

**Layout:**
- **Covers**: Dynamic composition with central figure, can include vignettes/panels
- **Pages**: Multi-panel grid layouts (e.g., 2x2, 3-panel strips)
- **Narration Boxes**: Rectangular cream background with black text and dark borders
- Dialogue embedded in panels

**Content Integration:**
- Integrate everything naturally: scenes, fuel-ups, drops, side quests, maps, cutscenes
- Stylized representations rather than hyper-realistic
- Energetic and dynamic compositions

## File Naming
- Cover: `cover.png`
- Pages: `page-01.png`, `page-02.png`, `page-03.png` (zero-padded, sequential)
- No episode numbers in filenames

## Export
Export only final images (cover + pages). No JSON, markdown, or metadata files.
